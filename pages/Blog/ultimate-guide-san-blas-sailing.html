Sailing in San Blas with a Private Bathroom

Your ultimate guide to comfort and privacy aboard a San Blas sailing tour.

Sailing the San Blas Islands of Panama is one of the most unforgettable experiences you can have — but choosing a cabin with a private bathroom takes the adventure to a whole new level of comfort and convenience.

The San Blas archipelago is a remote paradise of over 365 islands with turquoise waters, tropical beaches and authentic cultural experiences. When you choose a sailing trip with a private bathroom cabin aboard Lady Lou or another sailboat, you get privacy, comfort and a personalized experience that shared cabins can’t match.

Why a Private Bathroom Makes the Difference

Stepping aboard a sailboat in San Blas with your own bathroom means you won’t have to share facilities with strangers. This adds comfort on multi-day trips, more time exploring hidden islands, and less hassle during your adventure.

  • Full privacy during your trip
  • No waiting for facilities after sailing or snorkeling
  • Enhanced comfort for couples and families

What to Expect Onboard

Onboard a private cabin sailing experience, you’ll find quality amenities such as cozy beds, ample storage, fans and sometimes hot showers. Boats like *Lady Lou* offer cabins with ensuite bathrooms and personalized service aboard an intimate vessel.

Your sailing itinerary may include snorkeling in coral reefs, island hopping, relaxing on beaches, and immersion in Kuna culture — all while enjoying local meals prepared onboard.

"Choosing a private bathroom cabin turns your San Blas sailing trip into a more comfortable, intimate sailing getaway."

Whether you’re planning a couple’s getaway, a small group trip, or a special celebration, a private bathroom cabin gives you peace of mind and comfort as you explore one of the most secluded and beautiful destinations in Panama.
