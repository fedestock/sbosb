Sail & Jungle Adventure in San Blas

Discover the most complete San Blas sailing experience — blend sea, jungle, culture and adventure.

Why choose between sailing the Caribbean and exploring lush tropical jungles when you can have both? The Sail & Jungle Adventure is San Blas’ most complete experience — a journey where turquoise waters meet the wild beauty of Panama’s mainland.

This unique package blends comfortable sailing with a guided jungle trek, cultural immersion with the Kuna people, and snorkeling among coral reefs — all in one unforgettable adventure.

What the Sail & Jungle Adventure Includes

The Sail & Jungle Adventure combines:

  • Private or shared sailing aboard a comfortable sailboat (like Lady Lou).
  • A guided jungle tour with a local Kuna guide.
  • A traditional cultural lunch in a Kuna community.
  • Snorkeling, kayaking and exploring hidden beach coves.

Why This Adventure Is Special

This combination experience offers a perfect balance of:

  • Comfort: Stay aboard a sailboat with meals, cabins, and attentive service.
  • Culture: Meet the Kuna people and learn about their traditions.
  • Nature: Sail crystal-clear waters and trek through lush jungle landscapes.

Typical Pricing & Options

Prices vary depending on the type of sailboat and cabin:

  • Private boat with ensuite bathroom: ~USD 550/night (for 2 guests).
  • Private cabin with bathroom on a shared boat: ~USD 450/night.
  • Private cabin with shared bathroom: ~USD 380/night.
  • Jungle tour add-on (traditional lunch + local guide): ~USD 140 per group.

A Day in the Jungle & Sea

A typical Sail & Jungle Adventure day might look like this:

  • Morning: Wake up aboard your sailing boat and enjoy breakfast before getting picked up by your local guide for a jungle trek.
  • Midday: Explore rivers, waterfalls, and lush trails — then enjoy a traditional Kuna lunch.
  • Afternoon: Return to the boat to relax, snorkel or kayak in crystal turquoise waters.
  • Evening: Sail to a new island anchorage and enjoy the sunset from deck.

"The Sail & Jungle Adventure blends the sea breeze with the heartbeat of the rainforest — giving you both the freedom of sailing and the thrill of jungle exploration."
