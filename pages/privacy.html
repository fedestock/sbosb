Privacy Policy

San Blas on Sailboats is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, phone number, country of residence)
  • Reservation details (dates, package type, number of guests, preferences)
  • Payment-related information needed to process transactions
  • Any messages or communications you send us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process reservations and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and trip details
  • Improve customer service and internal operations
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not sell or share your personal information with unrelated third parties. Your data is only provided to partners involved in your reservation or when required by law.

4. Data Security

We apply technical and administrative safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, modification, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us through the official channels on our website.

7. Cookies

If cookies are used on our website, you will be clearly informed and given the option to accept them.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page.
